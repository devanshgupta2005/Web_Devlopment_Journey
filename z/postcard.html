Notes for Beginners

This profile card uses <article> to represent the whole card as one self-contained unit, <figure>/<figcaption> for the image, <address> for contact details, and both list types (<ul> and <ol>).

Since there is no CSS, the card will not look visually like a "card" — it will simply flow as plain, structured HTML content.
