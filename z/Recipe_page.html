Butter Paneer Masala

A rich, creamy North Indian curry made with paneer cubes simmered in a spiced tomato-butter gravy.

Prep15 min
Cook30 min
Serves4
LevelEasy

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a pan and sauté the chopped onion until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  4. Add red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add the paneer cubes and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Stir in the fresh cream and simmer for another 3 minutes.
  7. Garnish with coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
Vegetarian North Indian Curry Dinner
Tip: Soak the paneer in warm water for 10 minutes before adding it to the curry to keep it soft.
