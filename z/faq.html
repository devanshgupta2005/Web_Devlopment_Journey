Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

General

What services do you offer?

I build full-stack web applications, from responsive front-end interfaces to back-end APIs and database design, tailored to each client's needs.

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects take between two and six weeks depending on scope, from a simple landing page to a full application with authentication and a database.

Working Together

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes, all projects are handled remotely with regular updates over email or video calls, and progress shared through a shared repository or staging link.

What do you need from me to get started?
  • A short brief describing your goals
  • Any existing brand assets (logo, colors, fonts)
  • Examples of sites or designs you like
  • A rough timeline or deadline

Pricing

How is pricing determined?

Pricing depends on project scope and complexity. I provide a fixed quote after an initial consultation, so there are no surprises along the way.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes, maintenance and support packages are available after launch for updates, bug fixes, and small feature additions.
